How to Stay Motivated

Here are some strategies to help you stay motivated:

Short-term Goals

Set achievable goals for the next few weeks, like completing a specific number of workouts or hitting a personal best in an exercise.

Long-term Goals

Outline your broader objectives, such as running a marathon or building muscle over several months.

Consistency

Plan your workouts at specific times during the week and stick to them. Treat workouts like important appointments.

5 Tips for Better Sleep

Regular Sleep Times: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.

Wind Down: Develop a pre-sleep ritual that helps you relax, such as reading a book, taking a warm bath, or practicing mindfulness.
